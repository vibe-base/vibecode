Theme Toggle Demo

This is a simple demonstration of theme toggling functionality. Click the buttons above to switch between light, dark, and terminal themes.

Features

The theme toggle allows users to customize their experience with three different modes:

  • Light mode: Clean, bright interface for daytime use
  • Dark mode: Reduced eye strain in low-light environments
  • Terminal mode: Classic green-on-black for the nostalgic developer
